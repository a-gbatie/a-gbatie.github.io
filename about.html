Ange-Gaëlle is currently a Software Engineer with Cisco, her journey transitioning into the tech industry began in 2019. Ange's future plans include using her programming skills to create a software that can directly help with the nation's homeless crisis and other philanthropic endeavors. Outside of work you can find her listening to a true crime podcast, doing a jigsaw puzzle, reading, watching anime, or listening to music; she loves to travel and experience new cultures.
